A short biography

Oliver graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a bachelor's degree in economics and physics. Between 1995 and 2002, Oliver and his partners started three companies: Zip2, an online content publishing software, X.com, an electronic payment service, and PayPal. In June 2002, Oliver invested $100 million to launch spacex and served as Chief Executive Officer and Chief Technology Officer. In 2004, Oliver invested $6.3 million in Tesla Motors and became the company's chairman. In 2006, Oliver invested $10 million and co-founded Solar City, a photovoltaic power company.
